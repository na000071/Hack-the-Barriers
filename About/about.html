Mission Statement

Reduce barriers in tech hiring. Empower students to find accessible, inclusive opportunities.

We're committed to creating a level playing field where international students, permanent residents, and citizens can easily identify which opportunities are open to them. By eliminating the guesswork, we help students focus their energy on roles where they have a real chance to succeed.

Our Values
